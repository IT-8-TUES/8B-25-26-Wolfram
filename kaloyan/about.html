About Us

MYCO is a digital mushroom catalog dedicated to documenting fungal species from around the world through clean design, modern research, and accessible information.

Our goal is to help collectors, researchers, students, and curious explorers discover the fascinating diversity of mushrooms and fungi in an organized and visually engaging way.

Our Mission

We believe nature and science should be accessible to everyone. This catalog combines modern web design with educational fungal research to create a beautiful browsing experience.
